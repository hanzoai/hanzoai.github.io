Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?

No, there's no limit! You can refer as many people as you want and earn $5 for each successful referral.

When do I receive my referral credits?

Credits are added to your account automatically once your referred friend completes their signup and starts using their account.

Do my referred friends get anything?

Yes! Everyone who signs up gets $5 in free credits to start using Hanzo, whether they came through a referral or not.

What can I use the credits for?

Credits can be used for any Hanzo services, including AI compute, vector database usage, cloud deployments, and more.

How do I track my referrals?

You can track all your referrals and rewards from your account dashboard under the 'Referrals' tab.
